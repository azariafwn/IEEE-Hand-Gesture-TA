\section{Discussion and Limitations}
The experimental results demonstrate that the quantized LSTM model achieves a high balance between accuracy and computational efficiency. The minimal degradation in accuracy (0.25\%) despite a 97.7\% reduction in RAM usage validates that 8-bit quantization is a highly effective strategy for smart home applications on embedded hardware. 

However, this study identifies certain limitations. The gesture recognition performance is highly dependent on the hand landmark extraction quality of the MediaPipe framework, which can be affected by extreme occlusion or hands positioned outside the camera's field of view. Furthermore, the current model is trained primarily on data from a controlled environment, which may not fully represent the extreme variability of hand shapes and movements in a broader demographic.
